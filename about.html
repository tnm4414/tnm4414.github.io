About L N Unique Creations

Welcome to our shop! We are Neal and LaVerna, a married couple passionate about crafting custom gifts for all occasions. Located in the heart of Wilmington, our small shop allows us to give each project the personal touch it deserves. We are dedicated to helping you bring your imagination and designs to life, ensuring that every product is uniquely yours. Whether it's a gift for a loved one or a special item for yourself, we are here to make your vision set sail. Let us create something extraordinary for you!

We specialize in creating custom gifts with:

  • Laser Engraving
  • Sand Carving
  • Vinyl Lettering
  • Special Ink Products
